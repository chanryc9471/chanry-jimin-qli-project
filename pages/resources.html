= RESOURCES =

One of the ways we can help eliminate food waste all around is by donating uneaten food or left over food to organizations for them to put into community fridges. Grassroot Grocery does exactly this, a non-profit organization that distributes food to food pantries while also handing out food to those in need as well. Every Saturday, they take the time to distribute food to those that need it and help deliver uneaten food to different parts of the community. It's a special way to bond with those that are also helping others and a great act towards combating food waste. (Source: Grassroots Grocery)

Source: Grassroots Grocery

ReFed, another non-profit organization finds solutions to food waste in different ways. This website created by ReFed, allows users to interact with their database in which they collected from the internet. The website offers specific data on how food waste impacts different communities, environments, and places. The database had insights on specifically school food waste which also listed out the economic budgeting a school was wasting on school food waste as well as how much the federal government spends. The website offers various datasets and solutions on food waste in various different settings.

Source: ReFED
