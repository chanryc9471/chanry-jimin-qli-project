= ABOUT US =

We are both NYC public high school students working on a quality of life innovation proposal about school food waste. This website was our way of spreading awareness of the effects of school food waste that can negatively impact our community in ways people may not have thought of. We aim to allow students, teachers, and principals to see this website and take action in the quality of life issue of food waste.
